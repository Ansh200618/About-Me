## Next Steps - Deploy in 5 Minutes! ⏱️

### Option 1: GitHub Pages (Easiest - Recommended) ⭐

1. **Go to Repository Settings**
   - Navigate to: https://github.com/Ansh200618/About-Me/settings/pages

2. **Configure GitHub Pages**
   - Source: "Deploy from a branch"
   - Branch: Select "main" or "copilot/create-html-portfolio"
   - Folder: "/ (root)"
   - Click "Save"

3. **Access Your Live Site**
   - Your portfolio will be live at: `https://ansh200618.github.io/About-Me/`
   - Wait 2-3 minutes for initial deployment
   - Share this URL with anyone!

## Customize Your Portfolio

### Update Your Information

Edit `index.html` to customize:
- **Line 27**: Your name
- **Line 28**: Your title/role
- **Line 29**: Your current status
- **Lines 31-43**: Your social media links
- **Lines 59-62**: About me text
- **Lines 71-92**: Skills and tools

### Change Colors

Edit `style.css` variables at the top:
```css
:root {
    --primary-color: #00C4CC;  /* Change to your color */
    --secondary-color: #9999FF; /* Change to your color */
    /* ... more colors */
}
```
